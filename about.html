Hey! I'm Maggie,

a writer and media relations specialist originally from Long Island, New York. I now primarily reside in Montréal, Québec, where I've held several positions that have given me the opportunity to work on the public relations side of film. With a passion for queer cinema and low-budget indie movies, in addition to two years of experience working at a genre film festival, I'm always searching for the most innovative ways to get audiences interested in the films I care about.
